The plant grows as a woody shrub in dry, rocky soils across the northern regions of South Africa and has been used by Khoisan and Sotho communities for centuries. Global demand for Ashwagandha has surged dramatically since 2020, driven by the wellness industry's focus on stress, sleep, and cognitive performance. South Africa is uniquely positioned to supply a certified African-origin product to premium markets in Europe, the USA, and Asia. Research collaborations between South African universities and international pharmaceutical companies are actively investigating the local ecotype's commercial and therapeutic potential."
